Getting Started

How do I create a shopping list?

Tap the Lists tab, then tap the + button. Give your list a name, pick an icon and color, and optionally set a budget. Tap Save to create it.

How do I add items to a list?

Open a list and type in the text field at the top. Mise will suggest items you’ve added before. You can set a quantity, category, and price for each item.

How do I import a recipe from a website?

Two ways:

  • In the app: Go to Recipes, tap +, choose “Import from URL”, and paste the link
  • From Safari: Use the Share button and select Mise from the share sheet

Mise supports most recipe websites including AllRecipes, NYT Cooking, Serious Eats, and more.

How does meal planning work?

Go to the Meal Plan tab to see your weekly calendar. Tap any day to add a meal. You can link a recipe or just type a meal name. Plan breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snacks for each day.

Sharing & Sync

How do I share lists with my family?

Go to Settings and set up your Household. Once created, share the invite link with family members. After they join, toggle sharing on for any list, recipe, or meal plan.

Do I need an internet connection?

Mise works offline for all core features. Internet is needed for iCloud sync, household sharing, recipe imports from URLs, and weekly store deals.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. If you’re signed into iCloud, your data syncs automatically across all your Apple devices. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account — we never have access to it.

Weekly Deals

How do I see deals for my local stores?

Go to the Deals tab and enter your zip code. Mise will show weekly deals from stores near you. You can follow specific stores to filter the deals you see.

Can I add a deal to my shopping list?

Yes! Tap any deal and choose which shopping list to add it to. The item name and store info will be added automatically.

Troubleshooting

My data isn’t syncing between devices

Check the following:

  1. Both devices are signed into the same Apple ID
  2. iCloud is enabled for Mise (Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Mise)
  3. Both devices have an internet connection
  4. Try pulling down to refresh in any list

A recipe didn’t import correctly

Not all recipe websites format their content the same way. If an import is incomplete, you can edit the recipe manually. If you’d like us to improve support for a specific site, email us the URL at support@inceptyon.io.
