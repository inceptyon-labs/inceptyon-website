Mise Privacy Policy

Everything in its place — including your data

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Inceptyon Labs
The short version

Your data is yours. Mise stores your grocery lists, recipes, and meal plans on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see, collect, or sell your data. The only network call is to fetch weekly store deals. If you use voice search, audio is processed on-device by Apple — we never hear a thing.

1

Introduction

Inceptyon Labs LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) develops Mise (the “App”), a grocery list, recipe, and meal planning application for iOS and macOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2

Information We Do NOT Collect

Mise does not collect, store on our servers, transmit to us, or share with third parties:

  • Personal information (beyond what Apple Sign In provides for authentication)
  • Your shopping lists, recipes, or meal plans
  • Location data (GPS coordinates, precise location)
  • Device identifiers for advertising or tracking
  • Behavioral or advertising data
  • Analytics or usage telemetry
3

4

Household Sharing

Mise allows you to share lists, recipes, and meal plans with household members via Apple CloudKit Sharing. When you share:

  • Shared data is stored in a shared CloudKit zone accessible only to invited members
  • Sharing is managed by Apple’s infrastructure, not ours
  • You control who has access and can revoke sharing at any time
5

Third-Party Services

5.1 Weekly Store Deals (Flipp)

Mise fetches weekly store deals using the Flipp API. When you use this feature:

  • Data sent: Your zip/postal code (to find nearby stores)
  • Data received: Store names, deal descriptions, prices, and images
  • No personal identifiers are sent with these requests

You can choose not to use the deals feature. If you never enter a zip code, no data is sent to Flipp.

5.2 Sign In with Apple

Authentication is handled entirely by Apple. We receive only your name (if you choose to share it) and a relay email address. We do not store or transmit this beyond your local device.

Worth noting

No other third-party services. Mise has no advertising, no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no social media integrations.

6

Microphone & Speech Recognition

Mise can optionally use your device’s microphone and Apple’s on-device Speech Recognition framework to let you search for recipes by speaking ingredients aloud.

Entirely on-device

Audio is processed locally by Apple’s Speech framework. No audio data is sent to Mise’s servers or any third party.

Nothing is stored or recorded

Mise does not save, record, or retain any audio.

Always optional

This feature requires your explicit permission via the iOS microphone prompt. You can revoke access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

7

Other Device Data

Mise does not access your photos, contacts, camera, or any other device data beyond what is described above.

8

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data: Persists until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • iCloud data: Managed by your Apple ID settings. You can delete Mise data from iCloud via Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage
  • We retain nothing: Since we don’t collect your data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete
9

Children’s Privacy

Mise does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain advertising, social features, or user-generated public content.

10

Your Privacy Rights

Since Mise stores all data locally and in your personal iCloud account, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Deletion: Delete any item within the app, or uninstall to remove all local data
  • Portability: Your data lives in your iCloud account

For EU/EEA users (GDPR) and California users (CCPA): since we do not collect or process your personal data on our servers, these regulations’ data-subject rights are satisfied by default.
